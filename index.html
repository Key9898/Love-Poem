The sun is setting, Our love is glowing, The stars are twinkling, Our love is growing,
Your hand in mine, Your heart so kind, Our love is so divine, It’s too beautiful to define.

I’ve searched the skies, the deepest sea, For something pure to set me free.
Yet all along, it’s always been The way you smile, the way you’ve seen.
No diamond shines, no rose can bloom, Like you, who brightens every room.
Your voice, a song that calms the night, Your touch, a flame, a guiding light.
In crowded worlds, I just see two. My beating heart and only you.
No need for grand or distant stars. I’ve found the universe in ours.
I love you more than words can show, In ways I doubt you’ll ever know.
But still, I’ll write, I’ll speak, I’ll prove. With every breath, you are my love.
